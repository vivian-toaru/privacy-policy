Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Thank you for using VV Manga OCR ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of the images you choose to process. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Image Collection and Processing

User-Selected Images

The App allows you to import images from your device gallery or share images from other applications to extract text via Optical Character Recognition (OCR).

  • Explicit Action Only: We only access images that you manually select or share to the App. We do not index or scan your photo library in the background.
  • Purpose: To extract text (specifically Japanese manga dialogue) for your personal reference or study.
  • Local Processing: All image analysis is performed in real-time. Image data is held in volatile memory during the OCR process and is immediately released once extraction is finished.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do NOT store, archive, or upload your original images to any cloud servers or external databases.

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Names, email addresses (except for support), or phone numbers.
  • Device identifiers linked to your personal identity.
  • Social media account details or browsing history.

2. Permissions

To provide its services, the App requires the following permissions:

  • Storage / Media Access: Necessary to allow you to browse and select images from your device for processing.
  • Internet Access: May be used to download OCR model updates or utilize high-precision recognition APIs. All API communications are strictly encrypted via HTTPS.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use specialized AI text recognition services for enhanced accuracy with complex manga fonts.

  • Security: All selected image fragments are transmitted via secure, encrypted channels.
  • Anonymous Processing: No metadata identifying you or your device is attached to the transmitted image data.
  • Usage Restriction: Our API partners are prohibited from storing your images or using them for machine learning training.

4. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security protocols to ensure your data remains safe during the brief processing window. Because we do not store your images, there is no risk of a data breach revealing your personal content from our end.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The current version will always be available on this page.
