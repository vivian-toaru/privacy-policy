Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Thank you for using VV Manga Screen OCR ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our screen recognition services.

1. Information Collection and Usage

Screen Content

The App utilizes the Android MediaProjection API to capture screen content for Optical Character Recognition (OCR). This feature is triggered only when you explicitly tap the floating capture button or select an area on your screen.

  • Purpose: To extract text (specifically Japanese characters) from the selected screen area for your immediate use.
  • Processing: All image processing is performed in real-time within the device's volatile memory (RAM).
  • No Storage: We do NOT save, store, or archive your screenshots on your device storage or any external servers. The image data is permanently discarded immediately after text extraction is complete.

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • User names, addresses, or phone numbers
  • Contact lists or social media data
  • Location data (GPS)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address) linked to your identity

2. Permissions

To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:

  • Screen Capture (MediaProjection): Required to capture the image of the text you wish to recognize. You must grant this permission each time you start a recognition session unless you choose "Don't show again" (on supported Android versions).
  • Foreground Service: Required to keep the floating overlay active and ready for your input while you use other apps.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may utilize third-party OCR engines or translation APIs to provide high-accuracy text recognition.

  • Data Transmission: If cloud-based processing is required for enhanced accuracy, only the specific image crop you selected is transmitted securely via HTTPS.
  • Anonymity: Transmitted data contains no metadata that could identify you or your device.
  • Data Retention: Our third-party partners are contractually obligated not to use submitted images for training purposes or store them beyond the processing time required to return the result.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission and processing. Since we do not store your data, there is no risk of your screen captures being accessed from our servers in the event of a breach.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
